Jenny Xu

I am an undergrad in Computer Science at the University of Toronto. My research areas of interest are computer vision, machine learning and robotics. I am fortunate to work on an independent research project on the topic of Domain Adaptation in 3D Object Detection under the supervision of Professor Steven Waslander in Toronto Robotics and AI Lab at University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies. I am fortunate to be the Team Lead in 3D Object Detection at UofT Self-Driving Car Team.
