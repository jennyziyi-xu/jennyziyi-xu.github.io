Internship

From September 2021 to August 2022, I did a PEY Co-op Internship at The Center for Computational Medicine at Sickkids Hospital in Toronto. As a full-stack software developer, I worked with various frameworks including React, Flask, Express.js and MongoDB. We used Javascript, Typescript and Python as the main programming languages. I worked on three different projects.

  1. STAGER is a web application for information management for large-scale genomics projects in collaboration with CEHO (Children's Hospital of Eastern Ontario).
  2. OSMP (One-Sided Matching Portal) enables researchers to query patient variant and phenotype data from many different sources simultaneously. With two other software developers, I led the team to deploy a production instance of OSMP. Our collaborators include CHEO and Children's Mercy Hospital Kansas.
  3. I also had the chance to work on the design of a search engine for Zebrafish Enhancer Gene images.

It was satisfying to be able to contribute to something meaningful and make a positive impact on society.
