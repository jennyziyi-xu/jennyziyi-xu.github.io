CSSU Machine Learning Seminar
with Professor Roger Grosse 2020

Hi everyone,

Introducing our second machine learning seminar with Professor Roger Grosse! This will be a wonderful opportunity for you to learn about cutting-edge research, gain valuable advice about getting into research and discuss any ideas you have with one of the most famous professors in the Department of Computer Science at University of Toronto. Professor Roger Grosse's research focuses on deep learning and Bayesian modelling. If you have participated in the previous machine learning seminar with Professor David Duvenaud and undergrad research student Jacob Kelly, this will be a chance for you to gain new perspectives from another top researcher in machine learning at University of Toronto.

Join us: Friday, November 20th, 2020 @ 4-5 PM EST
