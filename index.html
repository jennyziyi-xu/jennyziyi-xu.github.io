Jenny Xu

I am an undergrad in Computer Science at the University of Toronto. My research areas of interest are autonomous driving, computer vision, machine learning and robotics . Robots are integrated into all aspects of our lives. They are becoming more intelligent and help humans accomplish tasks with high precision. With this trend, these autonomous systems will need to perceive the world at the human level. I am interested in using machine learning, computer vision and simulation techniques to improve 3D vision for autonomous vehicles and other robotic tasks.

I am fortunate to work on self-supervised 3D object detection under the supervision of Professor Steven Waslander in Toronto Robotics and AI Lab at University of Toronto Institute for Aerospace Studies. I am glad to have worked in Professor Nandita Vijaykumar's research group at the University of Toronto on real-time 3D mapping.
